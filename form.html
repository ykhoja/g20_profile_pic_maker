Welcome to my G20 Twitter Profile Pic Maker!

The app simply retrieves your current Twitter proifle pic, and adds the G20 Saudi Arabia ribbon to it. You can use it in 3 easy steps:
  1. Type your twitter ID below
  2. Click submit (or press enter) to generate your G20 profile pic
  3. Take a screenshot of the image and post it on social media

NOTE: You may experience some errors. For example, the app doesn't seem to work with emptry profile pics. Sorry for the inconvenience.
